Early Detection Assessment

This assessment is designed to help identify early signs of autism in children by focusing on key behavioral, social, and communication patterns. It is not a diagnostic tool but a simple, research-based questionnaire to guide awareness and early intervention. By answering a series of thoughtfully designed questions, you can gain insights into your child's developmental progress.

Your responses will remain confidential and will help us provide recommendations tailored to your child's needs. Early detection is crucial for timely support, fostering better outcomes for your child's growth and learning.

Take a few moments to complete the assessment, and together, let's work towards understanding and nurturing your child's potential.
